- Connect dialog no longer says "Leave empty to connect with SSH
  key" for profiles with a passphrase-protected key assigned —
  the field is now labelled "Key passphrase" and the hint reflects
  the actual key name. Previously entering nothing gave a silent
  JSch decrypt failure (#75).
- Enable Verbose SSH logging in Settings to see agent-forwarding
  diagnostics: identities registered/skipped, the full JSch
  identity repo contents, and each shell/exec channel that opens
  agent forwarding. Helps diagnose "ssh-add -l empty on remote"
  issues (#75).
